About Food Connect

Discover the story behind our platform and how we're connecting Loyola College students through food.

Where Friends and Food Meet

Food Connect is a student-focused platform created exclusively for Loyola College, Chennai. We believe that great food experiences are best shared with friends, and our mission is to make discovering and enjoying food around campus easier, more social, and more fun.

Founded by students for students, we understand the unique needs and challenges of campus life. Whether you're looking for affordable lunch spots, planning a group outing, or just want to share your latest food discovery, Food Connect is your go-to platform.

What We Offer

Discover Food Spots

Explore curated food places around Loyola College with detailed information, menus, and authentic reviews from fellow students.

Private Communities

Create exclusive groups with friends to plan food outings, share recommendations, and make collective decisions.

Interactive Polls

Vote on where to eat next with friends. Our polling system makes group decisions fair and fun.

Campus Map

Interactive map showing all food spots around campus with directions and distance information.

Our Mission

To enhance the college experience by creating a vibrant food community that helps students discover amazing food, connect with friends, and build memorable dining experiences around Loyola College.

  • Make food discovery easy and accessible
  • Foster social connections through shared food experiences
  • Support local food businesses around campus
  • Create a platform that grows with our student community

Our Vision

To become the essential food companion for every Loyola College student, transforming how students explore, share, and enjoy food experiences throughout their college journey.

Future Enhancements

  • Mobile app for iOS and Android
  • Integration with college events and festivals
  • Special student discounts and offers
  • Food delivery coordination
  • Culinary events and workshops
